Linear Magic Demystified



As you may have guessed it is not magic, simply math

More specifically algebra! Lets take a closer look at the operations being performed


Pick a number, lets say: 'x'

Double your number: 2x

Add 8 to your number: 2x + 8

Now divide by 2: (2x + 8)/2 = x+4

Finally, subtract your original number from this result: 4

So natuarally the final number you get is 4



We used algebra of linear terms: 'Linear ALgebra' to create magic!
